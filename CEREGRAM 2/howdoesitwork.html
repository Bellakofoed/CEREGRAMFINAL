Ceregram is a new social platform that analyses a photo, by scanning the pixels within the image. The app/website has the ability to enable what mood and emotion you are feeling when the photo has been taken.

Ceregram is an interactive social network that enables users to publically or personally post photos. When the photos are scanned via the individual pixels, the forebrain, frontal lope, cerebrum and most importantly the limbic system are assessed.

The most important part of the brain that analyses our mood is the limbic system, this is where ceregram bases their findings. By looking deeper into emotions within the brain, we have the ability to assess how happy one is compared to sad, an estimated mood at the time of the photo, as well as analysing their mental stablity.
